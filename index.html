EcoTravel

This app was developed during the electricity innovation challenge. It calculates how much carbon dioxide the user saves by taking the eletric bus in Gothenburg instead of taking a car. It also puts the result in a scoreboard, creating a competing element where the most environmentally friendly person is on top of the scoreboard.

A big part of the application is the ability to create and join your company and togheter get a good score. Our goal was to create a natural place for companies to challenge each other and show others how good they are at being environmentally friendly.
